About

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at NYU Courant, graduating in Fall 2027. I received my B.E. in Artificial Intelligence from Sun Yat-sen University.

During undergrad I worked with Prof. Zhenhui Peng and Prof. Xiaojuan Ma (HKUST) on HCI research — building LLM-powered assistance to augment user workflows and experience.

My current research interests center on:

① Self-improving agents — enabling LLMs and agents to continually learn, reason, and refine themselves in-context.
② Human-centered AI — strengthening LLM-driven interactive systems from the user's perspective.
